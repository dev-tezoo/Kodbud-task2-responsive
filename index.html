Our Mission

At WASSUP, our mission is to create a welcoming and inclusive community for runners of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just starting out, we believe that running is for everyone. Our goal is to inspire and motivate each other to reach our full potential, both on and off the track.
